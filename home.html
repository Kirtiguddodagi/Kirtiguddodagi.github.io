Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city located in the southern part of Maharashtra, close to Karnataka and Telangana.

It is known for spiritual centers, historic monuments, traditional festivals, and vibrant culture.

Famous places include Siddheshwar Temple, Pandharpur, and Akkalkot attracting millions of devotees.

Solapur is also famous for textiles like chaddars and towels.

Visitors experience spirituality, history, traditions, and delicious cuisine.
